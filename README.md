# Catch a Thief

## Context

A lil' fucker decided to commit a burglary in my building and steal some valuables in the appartment of my cousin. We managed to retrieve the  CCTV of the building (2 video streams), according to regulations and the approval of the landlords. I was tasked to isolate faces and the timestamps of the visitors to try and catch the thief.

Few problems :
- The burglary was comitted during a 2 weeks period, without any way of knowing when it started or ended.
- There are 2 video streams to analyze.
- The total files size is more than 640 GB.
- The entrances are used by at least 3 buildings of 15 floors. On top of that, a private school is located inside. ON TOP OF THAT, most of these buildings are being restored, so many entrances are always open and many workers are working on the site.

Realistically, I don't see how I could find the thief with thousands of faces to compare BUT, what makes this burglary unique is that only one appartment was targeted and it almost never happened before, according to the janitor. The door was also reinforced and only a few small (but valuables) items were stolen. Cousin's theory is that he knows the thief, which makes it a different story!
